Featured Riddles

🧠 The Missing Dollar
Three friends check into a hotel room that costs $30. They each pay $10. Later, the manager realizes the room is only $25, so he sends the bellboy with $5 to refund them. The bellboy pockets $2 and gives them $3 back. Each friend now paid $9 ($27 total) + $2 bellboy = $29. Where's the missing dollar?
There's no missing dollar! The $27 includes the $25 room + $2 bellboy tip.
🔥 Burning Rope
You have two ropes that each take exactly 1 hour to burn from end to end. However, they don't burn at a constant rate. How can you measure exactly 45 minutes?
Light both ends of one rope (30 min) and one end of the second. When the first finishes, light the other end of the second rope (15 min more = 45 min total).
